trois sœurs

about make contact

Jane, 7 - leader / planner

Jane keeps our projects on track - creating expense charts, wrangling the team and ensuring we stay on task. Even though she's the boss of the operation, Jane is never shy to suggest an ice cream break.

Ruby, 6 - maker / ideator

You'll find Ruby knocking on the door of the shop asking what we're building next. At bed time she likes to talk about all her new ideas and how they'll be made. Ruby is equally handy with the nail gun and the paint bush.

Lucy, 5 - builder / wildcard

Lucy is the baby of the group. There's no telling what she'll say or do next. Lucy loves painting, sneaking snacks and working with Dad. She's full of ideas and likes to let everyone know all about them.

Mama - teacher

Mom (Megan) is the lifeblood of this operation. Without her, none of this would be possible. She's passing on her skills of sewing, cooking and the ability to create great ideas to her three daughters.

Dada - teacher

Dad (Joe) has more than 20 years of experience creating all sorts of things - from buildings, to software applications to room sized video games. He loves nothing more than sharing his knowledge and experience with his three daughters.

Rufus - dog

He's our dog and he's such a good boy!
